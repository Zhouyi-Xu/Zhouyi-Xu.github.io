I am a master's student majoring in Computer Technology at Hangzhou City University. I received my bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Technology from Hangzhou City University.

Research Interests

My research lies in artificial intelligence, focusing on intelligent perception. 
I study perception problems across diverse data modalities and structures, with an emphasis on visual perception, 
3D understanding, and spatial reconstruction. More broadly, I am interested in building intelligent systems 
that progress from understanding to decision-making and creation.

My work spans:
- Visual perception (Computer Vision and 3D Vision)
- Spatial understanding and reconstruction (3D vision and computer graphics)
- Structured perception (graph-based modeling)
- Spatio-temporal perception (dynamic and sequential data)
- Multimodal perception (cross-modal understanding)

I am also interested in:
- Decision-making based on perception (e.g., reinforcement learning)
- Creation and simulation based on perception (e.g., generative modeling and computer graphics)

I am deeply interested in a broad range of topics within Artificial Intelligence. If you have thoughts on my research—or simply an exciting idea to share—I would love to hear from you. I look forward to sparking new insights and ideas through meaningful conversations.
